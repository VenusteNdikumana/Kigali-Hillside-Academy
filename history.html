Our History

Kigali Hillside Academy was founded in 2020 with the mission to provide high-quality education to the community in Kigali. Starting with a small group of dedicated educators, the academy has grown significantly, offering a range of programs from early childhood education through to secondary school. Over the years, our commitment to academic excellence and holistic development has earned us a reputation as one of the top schools in the region.

Our vision is to nurture future leaders through a well-rounded education that combines academic rigor, character-building, and global awareness. As we continue to expand, we remain rooted in our values of respect, integrity, and community involvement.
