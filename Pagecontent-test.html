Who we are

At KIGALI HILLSIDE ACADEMY, we provide a safe, engaging environment for children to learn and grow. Our experienced staff is dedicated to nurturing every child’s unique abilities. We believe in creating a nurturing and inspiring environment where every child feels safe, happy, and free to explore. Through play-based learning and hands-on activities, we guide children on a joyful journey that nurtures both their hearts and minds.


We pride ourselves on cultivating a sense of curiosity and wonder in every young mind that steps through our doors. Our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to encourage exploration, creativity, and problem-solving, ensuring that each child discovers their unique strengths and passions. We foster a love for learning that will serve as a foundation for a lifetime of growth and success.


With a strong emphasis on community, we strive to build strong partnerships with families, encouraging open communication and involvement. Our team works collaboratively with parents to support each child’s journey, ensuring a seamless and enriching educational experience. At KIGALI HILLSIDE ACADEMY, we are more than just a school; we are a family that grows together, learns together, and celebrates each child’s achievements.

We emphatize collaboration among learners with their respective teachers
